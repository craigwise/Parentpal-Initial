About ParentPal

Being a parent means we want the best for our children; and often, finding the best does result in a few online searches seeking out the most attractive deal. However, as parenting is a full time job in itself, we often don’t have the time to compare deals on every day products. Despite all the noise in the market place, parents still tend to purchase products that they love and trust, maintaining their brand loyalty. And while these brands are offered through a variety of different supermarkets and online retailers, there is often no store loyalty, provided they find a better deal elsewhere. As family budgets get tighter and time gets more constrained, ParentPal affords parents with a solution to save both time and money in finding the best deal on products and brands that they trust and use every day, without a compromise.
